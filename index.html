About the project

Hi 👋 I'm Mike and I'm obsessed with startups with no customers. I graduated from UC Berkeley and Carnegie Mellon. I've worked in innovation and entrepreneurship for the last 20 years.

Right now, I'm keen to speak with founders who raised seed funding recently and tried pivoting. I hope to learn about your experience.

Why we should meet

Benefits for you. The founders I've met appreciated processing their experience aloud with someone who cares.

Benefits for others. I want to know: Do pivoting founders struggle or have adequate resources? Our conversation will provide me with data to either help me avoid wasting my time or help other founders avoid wasting theirs.
